Opened in 2003, the Bar do Munhoz is located in the Talavasso neighborhood. The house offers customers a large space, with a relaxed and lively atmosphere, where it is possible to chat, meet people, flirt and enjoy day and night. The bar has a leisure room, including a games table and other entertainment, as well as large screens and televisions with state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems and a stage for live music.
