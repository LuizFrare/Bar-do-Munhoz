Everything you need to know about Bar do Munhoz


- What is the contact email for Bar do Munhoz?

Our contact email is info@munhozbar.com.br.

- Does the establishment make a table reservation?

Unfortunately we do not work with table reservation. Our queue is always on a first come, first served basis. Out of respect for everyone, we make no exceptions. It is possible to sip and snack while waiting for the table.

- Does the Bar accept food stamps?

Unfortunately we do not accept credit and debit cards.

- Does the place have a valet? What is the value?

We have outsourced valet service. The value is 10 reais.

- Is the command individual?

Our system of closing accounts is per table. Therefore, we do not have individual commands.

- Does Bar do Munhoz hold external events?

We do not do it, but we can supply our portions of crackling (same as the ones served in the bar but in a smaller size) for your event. It is necessary to pre-order, make the payment and withdraw at the bar on the day of consumption. The value of the cent (100 units) of crackling is 380 reais (already fried) or 340 reais (raw).

- Is it possible to reserve a part of the venue for an event?

Due to the movement, we were unable to attend private events.

- Does it deliver?

We no longer deliver. An alternative delivery is to place your order via delivery applications like Rappi and Spoonrocket.

- Do you have live music?

We work with live music and DJs.

- Which email do I send my resume to?

Your resume, with the position of interest, should be sent to the email Recursoshumanos@munhozbar.com.br or by the form located in "Contact Us".

- What is the price of food and drinks?

The price is located on the "Snacks" and "Drinks" pages. The price is in reais (BRL).
